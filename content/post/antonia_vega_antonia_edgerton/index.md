On September 16, 1947, Antonia C. Vega and Antonia Edgerton of Manitowoc, Wisconsin, patented a novel support system for kitchen containers. Their invention provided a wall-mounted canisters for powders and granules that were easy to fill, dispense, and remove while keeping a hinged lid open automatically.

Date of Application: 9 March 1945

Date of Issuance: 16 September 1947
