On September 27, 1910, Ava I. Chandler of Marion, Ohio, patented an adjustable holder for electric-light globes. Made of spring-wire rings joined by converging braces, her design securely gripped both socket and shade without screws and fit fixtures of various sizes.

Date of Application: 22 December 1909

Date of Issuance: 27 September 1910
