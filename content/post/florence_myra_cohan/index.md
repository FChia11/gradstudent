On November 20, 1923, Florence Myra Cohan of Oakland, California, California, patented a depilatory compound for permanently removing facial hair. Her invention included a two-part system: a wax-and-rosin mixture to remove hair and a separate lime-water-based solution to prevent regrowth and soothe the skin. 

Date of Application: 31 January 1917

Date of Issuance: 20 November 1923
