On November 26, 1906, Franziska Zuza of Vienna patented a method for making down-filled comforters using coated feathers. By blowing a mixture of down and powdered adhesive into the fabric and then activating the coating with heat or steam, her process allowed even filling while preventing dust loss and feather escape.

Date of Application: 2 May 1905

Date of Issuance: 26 November 1906
