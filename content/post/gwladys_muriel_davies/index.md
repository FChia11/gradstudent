On April 1, 1924, Nelle C. Flannery of Wooster, Ohio, was granted a patent for an improved apron design. Her invention featured a shaped front panel and cleverly integrated side pockets, making the apron more practical, durable, and flattering for everyday wear.

Date of Application: 15 September 1921

Date of Issuance: 1 April 1924
