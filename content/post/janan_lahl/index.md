On December 12, 1911, Janan Lahl of Atlantic, Iowa, patented an adjustable head-rest for bath tubs. Formed from a single piece of spring wire, the device gripped the tub securely while allowing a cushioned pillow to slide along the frame without screws or fasteners.

Date of Application: 15 December 1910

Date of Issuance: 12 December 1911
