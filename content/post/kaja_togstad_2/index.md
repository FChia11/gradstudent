On May 22, 1917, Kaja P. Togstad of Maddock, North Dakota, was awarded a patent for a wearable safety parachute for aviators that would automatically open if the pilot fell from the aircraft. Her device used a spring-loaded support attached to a belt to deploy the parachute and allow a safe landing. 

Date of Application: 21 February 1917

Date of Issuance: 22 May 1917
