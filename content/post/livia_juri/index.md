On September 15, 1947, Livia Juri of Lugano, Switzerland, received a patent for a novel container designed for preparing sweets. Her adjustable mold featured two interlocking sliding sections — secured by spring hooks or edge notches — allowing bakers to form desserts in multiple dimensions without needing multiple pans. 

Date of Application: 28 August 1946

Date of Issuance: 15 September 1947
