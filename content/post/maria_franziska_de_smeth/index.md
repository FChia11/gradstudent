On September 21, 1950, Maria Franziska De Smeth of Munich, Germany, patented a football-themed card game. Having eighteen cards with player positions and match situations, it could be played as a social game, a patience or oracle game, or as an educational sports game.

Date of Application: 14 June 1949

Date of Issuance: 21 September 1950
