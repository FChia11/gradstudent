On September 21, 1937, Nellie Victoria Linger of Kings Park, New York, patented an electric teapot designed to brew tea to a consistent strength. Featuring a perforated container with a teaspoon scale and a removable diaphragm, her design improved measuring and cleaning. 

Date of Application: 22 July 1936

Date of Issuance: 21 September 1937
