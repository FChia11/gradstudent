On February 6, 1917, Olivia W. Tucker of Brooklyn, New York, patented an adjustable ornamental fabric lamp shade. Designed with elastic and stay-filled bands, her shade could be fitted to fixtures of various sizes while maintaining a continuous, decorative appearance.

Date of Application: 16 September 1916

Date of Issuance: 6 February 1917
