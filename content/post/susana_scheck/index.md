On August 1, 1924, Susana A. Scheck of San Francisco, California, was awarded a patent for a novel garter. Her design used a flat plate with openings and prongs to secure the ends without sewing, allowing the wearer to adjust it easily. 

Date of Application: 2 May 1922

Date of Issuance: 1 August 1924
