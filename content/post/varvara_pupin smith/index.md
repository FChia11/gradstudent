On April 4, 1934, Varvara Pupin Smith of New York was awarded a patent for a novel lip guard. Her invention was designed to be worn while dressing, protecting garments from being stained by lip rouge.

Date of Application: 4 April 1934

Date of Issuance: 3 March 1936
